  • ResNeXt
  • Radical Trainer, a tiny flashcard app for learning common Chinese radicals.
  • Sentence Trainer, a flashcard app that shows an English sentence and reveals the Chinese characters and pinyin — 500 HSK 1 and 500 HSK 2 sentences, plus dialogue sentences from Integrated Chinese Lessons 1–20, studied lesson by lesson.
  • Word Trainer, a flashcard app that shows a Chinese word and reveals the pinyin, meaning and pronunciation (incl. a slow read) — Integrated Chinese Lessons 1–20 (split per dialogue), plus standalone Food, Common Verbs, Numbers and Tutor Notes topics.
  • Usage Trainer, a quiz for the judgment calls a tutor corrects — 你 vs 您, 几 vs 多少, 口 vs 个, spoken zhèi/nèi, adverb placement and the tone changes of 一 — with the rule explained after every answer.
  • Numbers & Time Trainer, a flashcard app that shows a number, a clock face or a digital time and reveals how to say it in Chinese (characters, pinyin and pronunciation) — numbers up to a million, weighted toward the small ones.
  • Colloquial Character Trainer, a flashcard app for the 500 most common Chinese characters (by frequency), with an example sentence, pinyin and pronunciation for each, plus mini usage lessons on the tricky grammar characters (的, 可, 了, 得, …) — study in sets of 20 (200 so far, more on the way).
