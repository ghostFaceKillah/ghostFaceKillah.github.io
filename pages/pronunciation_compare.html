Pronunciation: Option A vs Option B

Same 10 sentences (HSK 1 #1–10). Tap each button and compare how they sound.

Option A  Browser speech (Web Speech API). No files, no cost — the voice comes from your device, so quality varies and it may be silent if you have no Chinese voice installed.

Option B  Pre-generated audio (committed MP3s, neural TTS). Same on every device, works offline once loaded — costs repo space and a build step.

⚠ No Chinese (zh) voice found on this device, so Option A will be silent here. This is exactly the inconsistency that Option B avoids.

Option B clips generated with sherpa-onnx (MeloTTS, offline neural) via tools/gen_pronunciation_demo.py. On a normal network, edge-tts or Google Cloud TTS sound even better.
